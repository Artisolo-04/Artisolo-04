### 🌱 My story

I was born on **September 21, 2004**, and for as long as I can remember I've been someone who needs to *make* things, not just think about them.

It started with **traditional sketching and painting**, then **sculpting with clay**. Eventually I was building things on my own at home — DIY projects out of cardboard, just to see if I could turn an idea into something physical.

A few years later I discovered **technology**: robotics and **Arduino** were my entry point. That curiosity pulled me toward **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, and I started building my first websites.

Around the same time, I fell in love with **design** — I picked up **Illustrator** and **Photoshop**, moved into **3D modeling with Blender**, and then into **motion graphics and animation with After Effects**.

Today, all of that has converged into one big goal: becoming a strong **full-stack web developer**, without losing the creative, hands-on instinct that started this whole journey.
